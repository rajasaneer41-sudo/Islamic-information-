Surah Al-Fatiha

Arabic

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
مَالِكِ يَوْمِ الدِّينِ
إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ
اهْدِنَا الصِّرَاطَ الْمُسْتَقِيمَ
صِرَاطَ الَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ
غَيْرِ الْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا الضَّالِّينَ

English

In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful. All praise is for Allah, Lord of all worlds. The Most Gracious, the Most Merciful. Master of the Day of Judgment. You alone we worship, and You alone we ask for help. Guide us to the straight path. The path of those whom You have blessed, not those who earned Your anger, nor those who went astray.

Surah Al-Baqarah

Arabic

الم
ذَٰلِكَ الْكِتَابُ لَا رَيْبَ ۛ فِيهِ هُدًى لِلْمُتَّقِينَ
الَّذِينَ يُؤْمِنُونَ بِالْغَيْبِ وَيُقِيمُونَ الصَّلَاةَ وَمِمَّا رَزَقْنَاهُمْ يُنفِقُونَ
وَالَّذِينَ يُؤْمِنُونَ بِمَا أُنزِلَ إِلَيْكَ وَمَا أُنزِلَ مِن قَبْلِكَ وَبِالْآخِرَةِ هُمْ يُوقِنُونَ
أُو۟لَٰئِكَ عَلَىٰ هُدًى مِّن رَّبِّهِمْ ۖ وَأُو۟لَٰئِكَ هُمُ الْمُفْلِحُونَ

English Translation

Alif Lam Meem.
This is the Book about which there is no doubt, a guidance for those conscious of Allah.
Who believe in the unseen, establish prayer, and spend from what We have provided them.
And who believe in what has been revealed to you, [O Muhammad], and what was revealed before you, and of the Hereafter they are certain [in faith].
It is they who are upon guidance from their Lord, and it is they who will be successful.

Roman English

Alif Lam Meem.
Zalikal kitabu la rayba feehi hudal lilmuttaqeen.
Allazina yu’minoona bil ghaibi wa yuqimoona as-salata wa mimma razaqnahum yunfiqoon.
Wallazina yu’minoona bima unzila ilayka wa ma unzila min qablika wa bil-akhirati hum yooqinun.
Ulaaa’ika ‘ala hudan mirabbihim wa ulaaa’ika humul muflihoon.
